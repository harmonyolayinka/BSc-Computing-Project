Welcome to LearnComputing!

LearnComputing is an educational platform designed for young girls, (specifically ages 10-11) to encourage them to learn about computer science.

My Mission

LearnComputing is dedicated to closing the gender gap in computing by empowering young girls with knowledge, confidence, and excitement for technology.

I aim to provide an enjoyable and supportive learning environment where every girl feels welcome and capable of achieving great things.

Hopefully, through the creation of more computing resources that engage young girls, we will slowly start to see more girls and women pursuing computer science.

What you'll find on LearnComputing

  • Engaging lessons on various computing topics that align with the UK curriculum
  • Quizzes and challenges related to the lessons in order to assess understanding and progress
  • Inspirational stories about some influential women in the computer science field
  • A safe learning space designed for young learners, offering a secure and encouraging platform to grow

Why learn computer science?

Computer science is about more than just coding - it's also about problem-solving, creativity, and bringing ideas to life.

Individuals with various different skills and interests are bound to find an aspect of computing that they enjoy.

And with all of the fast-moving technological developments we see today, it is becoming an increasingly important subject and it is beneficial for everybody to have some level of basic understanding of computers and computer science.
